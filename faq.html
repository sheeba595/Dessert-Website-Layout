Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

+Who is the owner of the dessert house?

Ishwarya Balamurugan is the Founder and Creative Director of the Dessert House, established in 2024. Today it is professionally managed in India.

+Are the brownies eggless?

Yes, we offer eggless variants for most of our cakes, pastries and brownies.

+How can I order?

You can:
  • Order directly from our website www.thedesserthouse.com and click Order Online
  • Order via Zomato or Swiggy
  • For custom, bulk, or corporate orders, write to us at Click Here or call us at +91987 6543210.

+How long do the brownies last?

Our brownies stay fresh for 4days if stored in a cool, dry place at ambient temperature

+How should I store your products?

  1. All products are baked/prepared fresh daily without preservatives.
  2. Shelf life is mentioned on product descriptions or labels (for packaged goods like breads, cookies, crackers).

+Can I microwave or heat the products?

  1. Breads; Should not be microwaved.
  2. Savouries: Can be grilled or oven-heated.
  3. Cakes and Brownies: Best enjoyed at room temperature.
  4. If refrigerated, remove 15-20 minutes before eating.

+Do you offer sugar-free or vegan or gluten-free products?

Not on our regular menu.

+Does your website also deliver on Sundays and public holidays?

The Dessert House works on all Sundays and holidays except under uncontrollable situations like bad weather or civil disorder like bandhs.
