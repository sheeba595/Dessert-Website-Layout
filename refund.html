Refund Policy

Note: This policy applies only to orders placed online at order.thedesserthouse.in.

Your Money is Secure

  • If your amount has been deducted via UPI, Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, or Wallet, please don't worry your payment is safe under Consumer Safeguarding Guidelines

Important Points

  • Sometimes, bank servers may delay confirmation of payment.
  • If payment is confirmed within 15 minutes, your order will be processed automatically.
  • If payment confirmation reaches us after 15 minutes, the system will reject the order and initiate a refund automatically.
  • Once initiated, refunds take 5-7 working days to reflect in your account (depending on your bank/payment provider).

Support and Queries

  • For refund-related support, please email us at: contact@thedesserthouse.in
